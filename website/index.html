FAQ

Common questions.

How does the AI know about my business?

During setup, you enter your services, hours, and FAQs. The AI uses this as its knowledge base to answer calls accurately. You can update it anytime.

Does it sound robotic?

No. We use the latest voice AI technology. Callers typically can't tell the difference. It handles natural conversation, interruptions, and complex questions.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

Yes. You just set up call forwarding from your existing number to your ELYVN number. Takes 2 minutes. We walk you through it.

What happens if the AI can't handle a call?

It transfers to you or your team immediately. You get a Telegram alert with the caller's info and what was discussed so you can pick up right where the AI left off.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. We also offer a 7-day free trial so you can see real results before paying.

How fast can I get started?

15 minutes. Sign up, enter your business info, and your AI receptionist is live. Most businesses start seeing results within the first week.
