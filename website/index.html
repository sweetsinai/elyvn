During setup, you enter your services, hours, and FAQs. The AI uses this as its knowledge base to answer calls accurately. You can update it anytime.

No. We use the latest voice AI technology. Callers typically can't tell the difference. It handles natural conversation, interruptions, and complex questions.

Yes. You just set up call forwarding from your existing number to your ELYVN number. Takes 2 minutes. We walk you through it.
