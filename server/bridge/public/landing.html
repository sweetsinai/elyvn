Frequently Asked Questions

How does ELYVN answer calls?
▼
ELYVN integrates with your existing phone system and uses AI to answer incoming calls instantly. When your team is busy or after hours, ELYVN steps in to greet callers, gather information, and route leads appropriately.
How long does setup take?
▼
Setup typically takes 15-30 minutes. We'll walk you through connecting your phone number, configuring your business details, and setting up your dashboard. You can be live within the same day.
Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
▼
No contracts required. We offer month-to-month billing so you can cancel anytime. We're confident you'll love ELYVN, and we believe in earning your business every month.
Can ELYVN book appointments directly into my calendar?
▼
Yes! ELYVN integrates with Cal.com, Google Calendar, and other popular scheduling tools. When a lead books, it goes straight into your calendar and you get an instant Telegram alert.
What if I need to customize how ELYVN responds?
▼
Our dashboard lets you customize call greetings, qualification questions, appointment availability, and more. If you need advanced customization, our support team is happy to help.
